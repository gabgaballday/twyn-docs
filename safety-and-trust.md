# Safety & Trust

> Twyn uses multi-layered safety systems to protect users, including automatic content moderation in 80+ languages, PII redaction, and progressive accountability.

## Safety Philosophy

Twyn is built on the principle that safety is not an afterthought — it is foundational. Every feature, from onboarding to messaging, is designed with user protection in mind.

## Multi-Layered Protection

Twyn's safety systems operate at multiple levels:

### Content Moderation
- **Automatic detection**: Harmful, abusive, or inappropriate messages are caught automatically across 80+ languages.
- **Multilingual support**: Moderation works in all supported languages, not just English.
- **Pattern monitoring**: Conversation-level patterns are monitored to detect escalating behaviour or manipulation.

### PII Protection
- **Automatic redaction**: Personal identifiable information such as email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, and financial details are automatically detected and redacted in conversations.
- **Off-platform redirect detection**: Attempts to move conversations off-platform (a common scam tactic) are detected and flagged.

### User Controls
- **Block and report**: Users can block or report any profile at any time.
- **Emergency data deletion**: Users can trigger immediate deletion of their data if they feel unsafe.
- **Progressive accountability**: Users who violate community standards face escalating consequences.

### Identity Verification
- **Real people only**: Multi-step verification including live selfie checks keeps out fakes and bots.
- **Age verification**: Selfie-based liveness checks verify users are real and of appropriate age.
- **Optional ID verification**: Users can verify their identity for an additional trust badge.

## What Twyn Does Not Do

- Twyn does not sell user data.
- Twyn does not use manipulative engagement loops.
- Twyn does not create fake scarcity or pressure-based paywalls.
- Twyn does not monetise personal information.

# About Twyn & Founders

> Twyn is a bootstrapped and community-funded AI dating app founded by Gaby Cox, built on the belief that algorithms should strengthen connection rather than manipulate it.

## Mission

Twyn's mission is to create a global space where people feel safe, seen, and connected. We believe meaningful connections should go deeper than a profile picture. Twyn is not anti-algorithm — it is pro-human algorithm. Using AI to understand not just what you look like, but who you truly are.

## What Twyn Stands For

- ✓ No data selling
- ✓ No manipulative engagement loops
- ✓ No fake scarcity or paywalls
- ✓ Inclusive design and language from the ground up
- ✓ Algorithms built for empathy, not addiction

## The Movement

Twyn is not just building an app; it is building a movement.

- A movement toward technology that protects emotional health.
- Toward dating that is mindful, safe, and inclusive.
- And toward algorithms that make us more human, not less.

Funded by a global community that believes love and data should never be for sale.

## Founder — Gaby Cox

**Gaby Cox** is the Founder & CEO of Twyn.

After experiencing the frustrations of traditional dating apps — catfishing, ghosting, and endless cycles of surface-level swiping — Gaby founded Twyn to challenge the way technology shapes relationships. Twyn is a community-funded movement that reimagines how AI can support people with honesty, safety, and emotional intelligence.

Gaby's background spans fashion and technology, with eight years at adidas as a Global AI Product Manager for Ranking and Search. That experience taught her how deeply technology influences human behaviour and the way people form trust.

Having lived in five countries and travelled through fourteen, Gaby's approach to building technology embraces global inclusivity and cultural understanding.

As a neurodiverse member of the 2SLGBTQIA+ community, Gaby's approach to building technology is both personal and intentionally inclusive.

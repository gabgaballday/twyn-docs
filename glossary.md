# Glossary

> Key terms and definitions used in the Twyn ecosystem, optimised for search engine understanding.

---

**AI Twin / Twyn**
A digital personality created from a user's onboarding conversations. The AI Twyn represents how a person communicates, what they value, and what matters to them in a partner. Other users can chat with someone's AI Twyn to preview compatibility.

**Compatibility Engine**
Twyn's matching system that analyses personality traits, values, communication style, and emotional maturity to surface genuinely compatible matches. It prioritises deeper compatibility signals over surface-level preferences.

**Highlights**
Compatibility insights shown before messaging a match. Highlights include "What You Share" (common values and traits), "Where You Complement Each Other" (constructive differences), and "Things to Be Aware Of" (potential friction points).

**Mirror Screen**
The screen shown after onboarding is complete, where a user first sees their AI Twyn and the personality insights captured from their conversations.

**Expression Language**
The language a user writes their responses in during onboarding and conversations. This can differ from the UI language (interface text), allowing users to express themselves naturally regardless of which language the app interface is displayed in.

**AI Twyn Chat**
A feature allowing users to chat with the AI Twyn of a potential match before deciding to like or pass. This previews compatibility and communication style in a low-pressure way.

**Scan to Match**
A QR code feature that allows users to check compatibility with someone in real life. The QR code can be displayed from the app or saved to Apple/Google Wallet.

**Twyn Together**
A companion product for existing couples, offering guided conversations, compatibility insights, and relationship growth tools.

**ImpactAPI**
An open-source SDK developed by Twyn Global that estimates the environmental footprint of AI API calls, including energy consumption, carbon emissions, and water usage per request.

**Season Pass**
A seasonal billing option where users pay a one-time fee for 3 months of Premium or Elite access, with no auto-renewal.

**Connection Insights**
Detailed compatibility analysis generated from two users' AI Twyn profiles, showing shared values, complementary traits, and potential areas of friction.

**Conversational Onboarding**
Twyn's approach to profile creation, where users have a natural conversation with AI instead of filling out forms. This captures personality, values, and communication style more authentically.

**Progressive Accountability**
Twyn's moderation system where users who violate community standards face escalating consequences, from warnings to temporary restrictions to permanent bans.

**Personal Vault**
The encrypted storage system where sensitive user data — including personality profiles and special category data — is kept. Users control access and can delete vault contents at any time.

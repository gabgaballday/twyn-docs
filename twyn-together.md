# Twyn Together

> Twyn Together is a companion product designed to help existing couples strengthen their relationship through guided conversations, compatibility insights, and shared growth tools.

## Overview

While Twyn helps people find compatible partners, **Twyn Together** is built for people who are already in a relationship. It uses the same personality-understanding technology to help couples deepen their connection, navigate challenges, and grow together.

## Key Features

- **Guided conversations**: Structured conversation prompts designed to help couples explore topics they might not discuss naturally
- **Compatibility insights**: Understanding of where partners align and where they differ
- **Conflict resolution tools**: Guided approaches to navigating disagreements constructively
- **Shared growth tracking**: Tools for couples to track their relationship journey
- **Partner linking**: Securely connect with your partner within the app

## How It Differs from Twyn

| Feature | Twyn | Twyn Together |
|---------|------|---------------|
| **Purpose** | Finding compatible partners | Strengthening existing relationships |
| **Users** | Singles | Couples |
| **AI Twin usage** | Matching and compatibility preview | Understanding partner dynamics |
| **Conversations** | With potential matches | With your partner (guided) |

## Privacy

- All relationship data is shared only between linked partners
- Either partner can unlink at any time
- Data deletion applies to both individual and shared data
- The same encryption and privacy protections apply as in the main Twyn app

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do both partners need to use Twyn Together?**
A: Yes. Both partners create accounts and link together to access shared features.

**Q: Can I use Twyn and Twyn Together at the same time?**
A: The apps serve different purposes. Twyn is for finding new connections; Twyn Together is for existing relationships.

**Q: Is Twyn Together free?**
A: Twyn Together has its own subscription tiers. Check the app for current pricing.

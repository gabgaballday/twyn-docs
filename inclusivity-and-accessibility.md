# Inclusivity & Accessibility

> Twyn supports 81 languages, is inclusive of the full 2SLGBTQIA+ spectrum, and meets WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards.

## Language Support

Twyn supports **81 languages** across all major world regions:

### By Region

| Region | Languages |
|--------|-----------|
| **English variants** | Australian, British, Canadian, Irish, New Zealand, US English |
| **European** | Bulgarian, Catalan, Czech, Welsh, Danish, German, Greek, Spanish (Spain & Mexico), Estonian, Basque, Finnish, French (France & Canada), Irish, Croatian, Hungarian, Icelandic, Italian, Lithuanian, Latvian, Maltese, Dutch, Norwegian (Bokmål & Nynorsk), Polish, Portuguese (Brazil & Portugal), Romanian, Slovak, Slovenian, Albanian, Serbian, Swedish |
| **Middle East & Central Asia** | Arabic, Persian, Hebrew, Armenian, Georgian, Turkish |
| **South Asian** | Bengali, Gujarati, Hindi, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Nepali, Odia, Punjabi, Sinhala, Tamil, Telugu, Urdu |
| **East & Southeast Asian** | Indonesian, Japanese, Khmer, Korean, Lao, Mongolian, Malay, Burmese, Thai, Tagalog, Filipino, Vietnamese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional) |
| **African** | Afrikaans, Amharic, Hausa, Māori, Somali, Swahili, Yoruba |
| **Other** | Russian, Ukrainian |

Twyn distinguishes between **UI language** (interface text) and **expression language** (the language users write their responses in), allowing people to use the app in one language while expressing themselves in another.

## 2SLGBTQIA+ Inclusive Design

Twyn treats gender identity, romantic orientation, and sexuality as separate, personal dimensions:

- Users can describe their identity in their own words — not just from a predefined list
- Full privacy controls over who sees what aspects of your identity
- Sign language communities (ASL, BSL, and others) are treated as a real linguistic identity you can match on
- The matching engine does not impose heteronormative defaults

## Accessibility

Twyn is designed to meet **WCAG 2.1 Level AA** compliance:

- **High contrast mode**: Available for users with visual impairments
- **Reduced motion support**: Animations can be disabled for users sensitive to motion
- **Screen reader compatibility**: Core flows are accessible via assistive technology
- **Keyboard navigation**: All primary actions are keyboard-accessible

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use Twyn in my language?**
A: Twyn supports 81 languages. If your language is not yet supported, regional expansion is ongoing, with focus on African and South American languages.

**Q: How does Twyn handle gender identity?**
A: Gender identity, romantic orientation, and sexuality are treated as separate dimensions. Users can express these in their own words with full privacy controls.

**Q: Is Twyn accessible for visually impaired users?**
A: Yes. Twyn supports high contrast mode, reduced motion, screen readers, and keyboard navigation in line with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards.

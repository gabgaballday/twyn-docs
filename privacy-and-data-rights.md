# Privacy & Data Rights

> Twyn does not sell or share user data. Sensitive information is encrypted in a personal vault, and users have full control over their data.

## Data Philosophy

Twyn's approach to data is simple: your data belongs to you. It is never sold, never shared with third parties for advertising, and never used to train external AI models.

## Privacy by Design

- **Encrypted vault**: Sensitive personality and identity data is encrypted and stored in a personal vault
- **Consent at every step**: Users explicitly consent to each type of data collection, and consent can be revoked at any time
- **Minimal data retention**: Only data necessary for the service is retained
- **No ad-based monetisation**: Twyn's business model is subscription-based, not advertising-based

## User Data Rights

Twyn respects data rights across all jurisdictions where it operates:

| Jurisdiction | Regulation | Key Rights |
|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **UK / EU** | GDPR | Access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection |
| **California, USA** | CCPA / CPRA | Know, delete, opt-out of sale, non-discrimination |
| **Australia** | Privacy Act 1988 | Access, correction, complaint |
| **Canada** | PIPEDA | Access, correction, consent withdrawal |
| **Brazil** | LGPD | Access, correction, deletion, portability |

## Special Category Data

Some data Twyn collects — such as sexual orientation, gender identity, and relationship preferences — is classified as special category data under GDPR. Twyn handles this with additional protections:

- Explicit, informed consent is required before collection
- Consent can be revoked at any time
- Special category data is stored with additional encryption
- Processing is limited to the specific purposes consented to

## Account & Data Deletion

- **In-app deletion**: Users can delete their account and all data from Settings
- **Emergency deletion**: Immediate data deletion is available if a user feels unsafe
- **Deletion request form**: Available at [twynapp.co.uk/delete-account](https://twynapp.co.uk/delete-account)
- **Data export**: Users can request a copy of all their data
- **Processing time**: Deletion requests are processed within 30 days

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does Twyn sell my data?**
A: No. Twyn does not sell, share, or monetise user data in any way.

**Q: Can I delete all my data?**
A: Yes. You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app, or submit a request at twynapp.co.uk/delete-account.

**Q: Does Twyn use my data to train AI?**
A: Twyn does not use your data to train external AI models.

**Q: What data does Twyn encrypt?**
A: Sensitive personality, identity, and special category data is encrypted and stored in a personal vault.

# What is Twyn?

> Twyn is an AI dating app that creates a digital version of you — your AI Twin — through natural conversation, then uses a compatibility engine to find genuinely compatible people.

## Overview

Twyn is a dating app that takes a fundamentally different approach to matching. Instead of relying on photos and swiping, Twyn builds an AI profile of each user through a relaxed, conversational onboarding experience. This AI profile — called a "Twyn" — learns how you communicate, what you value, and what matters most to you in a partner.

Once your Twyn is created, it works on your behalf to find compatible matches. You can chat with the AI Twyns of other profiles before committing to real conversations, so you only spend time on connections worth having.

## How It Works

1. **Conversational onboarding**: Instead of filling out forms, you have a natural conversation with Twyn's AI. It asks about your values, communication style, and what you're looking for — in your own words.

2. **AI Twin creation**: From your responses, Twyn builds a digital personality mirror — your AI Twin. This represents how you communicate and what matters to you.

3. **Compatibility matching**: Twyn's compatibility engine analyses personality traits, values, communication style, and emotional compatibility to surface your best matches.

4. **AI Twyn conversations**: Before messaging someone directly, you can chat with their AI Twyn to preview compatibility and get a sense of how they communicate.

5. **Connection insights**: Before you ever message someone, Twyn shows you what you have in common, where you complement each other, and anything worth being aware of.

## What Makes Twyn Different

- **Personality-first matching**: Twyn matches you based on shared values, communication style, and emotional compatibility — not just looks or swiping habits.
- **AI as a bridge, not a replacement**: AI is used to enhance the journey to genuine human connection, not to replace it.
- **Connection insights before conversation**: Detailed compatibility highlights are available before you message anyone.
- **No data selling**: Twyn does not sell or share user data. Ever.
- **81 languages**: Full UI localisation across 81 languages, including right-to-left scripts and indigenous languages.
- **Inclusive by design**: Gender identity, sexuality, and pronouns are treated as personal dimensions with full privacy controls.

## Who Is Twyn For?

Twyn is for anyone seeking meaningful romantic connection who is tired of surface-level swiping. The app is designed to be inclusive of all orientations, gender identities, relationship styles, and cultural backgrounds.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Twyn free to use?**
A: Yes. Twyn offers a free tier with 50 AI Twyn conversations per day, unlimited messaging with matches, and basic compatibility highlights. Premium and Elite tiers are available for deeper features.

**Q: How is Twyn different from other dating apps?**
A: Twyn creates a digital version of your personality and uses it to find genuinely compatible people. You can preview compatibility through AI conversations before committing to real ones.

**Q: Is my data safe with Twyn?**
A: Yes. Twyn does not sell or share user data. Sensitive information is encrypted and stored in a personal vault. Users have full control over what they share.

**Q: What languages does Twyn support?**
A: Twyn supports 81 languages across all major regions, including right-to-left scripts, South East Asian languages, and indigenous languages.

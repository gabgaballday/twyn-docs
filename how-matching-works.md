# How Matching Works in Twyn

> Twyn uses a personality-first compatibility engine that weighs values, communication style, and emotional maturity — not just photos or swiping behaviour.

## Matching Philosophy

Twyn's matching system is built on the principle that lasting compatibility comes from shared values, complementary communication styles, and emotional alignment. Unlike traditional dating apps that prioritise visual attraction and behavioural signals (like swipe patterns), Twyn prioritises who you are over what you look like.

## What Twyn Evaluates

Twyn's compatibility engine considers multiple dimensions of personality and values:

- **Personality traits**: How you think, feel, and interact with others
- **Communication style**: How you express yourself and what kind of communication you respond to
- **Core values**: What principles guide your life decisions
- **Emotional maturity**: How you handle conflict, vulnerability, and growth
- **Language and expression**: How you articulate your thoughts and feelings
- **LGBTQ+ journey**: For users who choose to share, where they are in their identity journey

These dimensions are weighted to prioritise deeper compatibility signals over surface-level preferences.

## Geographic Matching

Twyn uses a tiered geographic approach to surface matches:

1. **Local**: Matches in your immediate area are prioritised first
2. **National**: The pool expands regionally and nationally
3. **Global**: For users open to long-distance or international connections

## Compatibility Insights

Before messaging a match, users can view detailed compatibility insights that include:

- **What you share**: Common values, interests, and personality traits
- **Where you complement each other**: Differences that could strengthen the relationship
- **Things to be aware of**: Potential friction points presented constructively

These insights are generated through AI analysis of both users' personality profiles, helping people make informed decisions about who to invest time in.

## AI Twyn Conversations

Users can chat with the AI Twyn of a potential match before deciding to like or pass. This allows them to:

- Ask questions about the other person's values and lifestyle
- Get a sense of how the person communicates
- Preview compatibility in a low-pressure way

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does Twyn use a percentage-based match score?**
A: Twyn focuses on detailed compatibility insights rather than reducing compatibility to a single number. Users see what they share, where they complement each other, and what to be aware of.

**Q: Can I match with people in other countries?**
A: Yes. Twyn uses geographic tiers starting with local matches and expanding to national and global pools based on your preferences.

**Q: How does Twyn avoid bias in matching?**
A: Twyn's matching engine prioritises personality and values over demographic signals. The system is designed to surface genuine compatibility rather than reinforcing superficial preferences.

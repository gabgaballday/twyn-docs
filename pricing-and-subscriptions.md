# Pricing & Subscriptions

> Twyn offers three tiers: Free, Premium (£14.99/month), and Elite (£24.99/month). Seasonal and annual billing options save 20%.

## Subscription Tiers

### Free
- 50 AI Twyn conversations per day
- Unlimited profile viewing and liking
- Unlimited messaging with matches
- Basic compatibility highlights
- Standard profile features

### Premium — £14.99/month
- 300 AI Twyn conversations per day
- Unlimited profile viewing and liking
- Unlimited messaging with matches
- Full connection insights
- Full compatibility analysis
- All Free features

### Elite — £24.99/month
- Unlimited AI Twyn conversations
- Unlimited profile viewing and liking
- Unlimited messaging with matches
- Full connection insights
- Full compatibility analysis
- Beta access to new features
- All Premium features

## Billing Options

| Billing Period | Premium | Elite |
|----------------|---------|-------|
| **Monthly** | £14.99/month | £24.99/month |
| **Seasonal** (3-month one-time) | £35.98 (one-time) | £59.98 (one-time) |
| **Annual** | £11.99/month (£143.88/year) | £19.99/month (£239.88/year) |

Seasonal and annual billing options offer **20% savings** compared to monthly billing.

## Key Details

- **Free to download**: The app is free to download and use with the Free tier.
- **In-app subscription management**: Subscriptions are managed through Apple App Store or Google Play.
- **Cancel anytime**: Users receive a reminder email before renewal and can cancel at any time.
- **No manipulative paywalls**: Human-to-human messaging and profile viewing are unlimited on all tiers, including Free.
- **Seasonal billing**: A one-time payment for 3 months with no auto-renewal.

## Multi-Currency Support

Twyn displays pricing in 18 currencies including GBP, EUR, USD, AUD, CAD, CHF, INR, JPY, KRW, and more. Prices are converted at current exchange rates.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's included in the free tier?**
A: The free tier includes 50 AI Twyn conversations per day, unlimited messaging with matches, unlimited profile viewing, and basic compatibility highlights.

**Q: What's the difference between Premium and Elite?**
A: Premium offers 300 AI Twyn conversations per day with full insights. Elite offers unlimited conversations plus beta access to new features.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes. You can cancel at any time through Apple App Store or Google Play. A reminder email is sent before renewal.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: The Free tier itself provides full access to core features. Premium and Elite unlock higher conversation limits and deeper compatibility analysis.

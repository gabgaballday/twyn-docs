# AI Twin Explained

> Your AI Twin (or "Twyn") is a digital personality mirror created from your onboarding conversations. It represents how you communicate and what matters to you.

## What Is an AI Twin?

An AI Twin is a digital representation of your personality, values, and communication style. It is created through Twyn's conversational onboarding process — not through forms or questionnaires, but through natural dialogue.

Your AI Twin learns:

- How you express yourself
- What you value in relationships
- Your communication preferences
- What matters most to you in a partner

## How Is It Created?

During onboarding, Twyn's AI engages you in a relaxed conversation. It asks open-ended questions about your values, lifestyle, and relationship expectations. From your responses, it builds a personality profile that captures not just what you said, but how you said it.

This conversational approach means your AI Twin reflects your authentic self, not a curated version of what you think sounds good on a profile.

## What Can Your AI Twin Do?

- **Represent you in compatibility matching**: Your Twyn is analysed against other users' Twyns to find genuine compatibility.
- **Chat on your behalf**: Other users can chat with your AI Twyn to preview what a conversation with you might be like.
- **Generate insights**: Your Twyn helps produce compatibility reports showing what you share with a potential match and where you differ.

## Privacy and Ownership

- Your AI Twin is yours. It is not shared, sold, or used for purposes other than matching.
- Sensitive personality data is encrypted and stored in a personal vault.
- You can delete your AI Twin and all associated data at any time.
- Twyn does not use your data to train external AI models.

## Why an AI Twin?

Traditional dating apps reduce people to photos and short bios. Twyn's AI Twin approach captures the depth of who someone is — their values, humour, emotional intelligence, and communication style — so matches are based on genuine compatibility rather than surface impressions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I see what my AI Twin says about me?**
A: Yes. After onboarding, you can view your personality insights, communication style, and other attributes your AI Twin has captured.

**Q: Can other people see my full AI Twin profile?**
A: No. Other users can chat with your AI Twyn and see compatibility highlights, but they cannot see your raw personality data.

**Q: Can I update my AI Twin?**
A: Your AI Twin evolves as you use the app and provide more information. You can also redo parts of the onboarding conversation.

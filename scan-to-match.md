# Scan to Match

> Twyn's Scan to Match feature lets you check compatibility with someone in real life by scanning a QR code — at events, bars, or social gatherings.

## What Is Scan to Match?

Scan to Match is a feature that bridges Twyn's digital compatibility engine with real-world interactions. Each user has a unique QR code that others can scan to instantly check compatibility.

## How It Works

1. **Open your QR code**: From your profile, tap "Scan to Match" to display your personal QR code.
2. **Someone scans it**: Another Twyn user scans your code using their phone's camera or the Twyn app.
3. **Instant compatibility check**: Both users can see compatibility insights — what they share, where they complement each other, and any highlights.
4. **Connect if interested**: If both are interested, they can match and start a conversation directly.

## Add to Wallet

Users can save their Scan to Match QR code to their **Apple Wallet** or **Google Wallet** for quick access. This means you don't even need to open the app — just show your wallet pass.

## Use Cases

- **Social events and parties**: Meet someone interesting and instantly check if you're compatible
- **Bars and restaurants**: A natural conversation starter
- **Speed dating events**: Replace traditional awkward introductions with compatibility-informed connections
- **Conferences and meetups**: Networking meets dating in professional settings

## Privacy

- Scanning only reveals compatibility highlights — not your full personality profile
- You can disable Scan to Match at any time from settings
- No location data is shared through the QR code

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do both people need to have Twyn installed?**
A: Yes. Both users need Twyn accounts for the compatibility check to work.

**Q: Can someone track me through my QR code?**
A: No. The QR code contains no location data. It only initiates a compatibility check within the app.

**Q: Can I turn off Scan to Match?**
A: Yes. You can disable the feature at any time from your profile settings.

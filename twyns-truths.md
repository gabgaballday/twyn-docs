# Twyn's Truths

> Six core principles that guide everything Twyn builds and every decision it makes. These are commitments, not just values.

## Introduction

At Twyn, we believe that technology should serve humanity, not the other way around. These six truths are more than values; they are commitments. They shape every line of code, every design decision, and every conversation we have with our community.

This isn't just about building a better dating app. It's about building a better future for how technology connects us, respects us, and empowers us.

---

## 1. Design for Real Lives

At Twyn, we believe technology must always serve the human first. Unlike many companies that design primarily for internal efficiency or metrics — for example by optimising purely for ad revenue — we design for real lives and real emotions. We acknowledge that many platforms are built around what is easiest for the company, but we are committed to creating a space where technology adapts to people rather than the other way around.

## 2. Champion Every Human Story

We know that many platforms treat inclusivity and privacy as afterthoughts. At Twyn, these principles are at the forefront. While some companies may overlook accessibility or fail to consider the needs of diverse communities, we ensure that our platform is welcoming and safe for everyone. We explicitly stand by diversity, equity, and inclusion. We believe that reflecting the diversity of the world in our team and our product is not just the right thing to do but essential to serving our users authentically.

## 3. Let AI Elevate Humanity

In an industry where AI is sometimes used to fully replace human interaction, we take a more balanced approach. Twyn uses AI intentionally as a bridge to enhance and streamline the dating experience, not to replace genuine human connection. While other platforms may rely on AI to carry the entire conversation, we use it to help people skip repetitive small talk and get to the heart of meaningful human connections more quickly. Our AI is there to enhance the journey and save time, ensuring that the ultimate goal is a genuine human relationship.

## 4. Protect People Relentlessly

We recognise that many companies treat user data as a commodity and accept data breaches as the cost of doing business. At Twyn, we refuse to compromise on privacy or security. While some platforms monetise personal information, use weak identity checks, or rely on manipulative paywalls that prey on vulnerability, we take the opposite approach. We protect every piece of user data with the highest standards of security and never sell or misuse information. Our commitment is to safeguard people both inside and outside the app.

## 5. Use Success to Lift Society

We believe that success carries responsibility. While some companies focus solely on profit, we are committed to using Twyn's growth to drive real-world change. A significant portion of our profits will go toward ending homelessness and supporting fundamental human dignity, because we believe stability and safety should be universal. Our goal is to show that a for-profit company can succeed without exploiting people.

## 6. Build the Future We Deserve

In a tech landscape often driven by scale, speed, and profit at any cost, we choose a different path. Twyn exists to prove that ethical innovation is not only possible but powerful. We want our team, our community, and the wider industry to feel inspired by a company that leads with integrity and puts people first. Twyn is a call to everyone who believes technology can be kinder, safer, and more human.

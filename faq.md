# Frequently Asked Questions

> Comprehensive FAQ covering Twyn's features, subscriptions, verification, privacy, and data rights.

## General

**Q: What is Twyn?**
A: Twyn is an AI dating app that creates a digital version of your personality — your AI Twin — through natural conversation. Your Twyn learns how you communicate and what you value, then works on your behalf to find genuinely compatible people.

**Q: How is Twyn different from other dating apps?**
A: Twyn matches you based on personality, values, and communication style — not just photos. You can chat with AI Twyns of other profiles before committing to real conversations, so you only spend time on connections worth having.

**Q: What languages does Twyn support?**
A: Twyn supports 81 languages across all major world regions, including right-to-left scripts, South East Asian languages, and indigenous languages.

**Q: Is Twyn available worldwide?**
A: Twyn is designed for global availability. The app uses geographic matching tiers starting with local matches and expanding nationally and globally.

## Subscriptions & Pricing

**Q: Is Twyn free?**
A: Yes. The Free tier includes 50 AI Twyn conversations per day, unlimited messaging with matches, and basic compatibility highlights. Premium (£14.99/month) and Elite (£24.99/month) tiers offer more features.

**Q: What billing options are available?**
A: Monthly, Seasonal (3-month one-time payment), and Annual. Seasonal and annual options save 20%.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, anytime through Apple App Store or Google Play. A reminder email is sent before renewal.

**Q: What's included in Premium?**
A: 300 AI Twyn conversations per day, full connection insights, full compatibility analysis, and all Free features.

**Q: What's included in Elite?**
A: Unlimited AI Twyn conversations, beta access to new features, and all Premium features.

## Verification

**Q: How does age verification work?**
A: Twyn uses a selfie liveness check to verify users are real and of appropriate age. The verification provider retains data for 30 days then deletes it.

**Q: Do I need to verify my identity?**
A: Age verification via selfie is required. Full ID verification is optional but adds a trust badge.

**Q: What authentication methods are available?**
A: Email, phone OTP, Apple Sign-In, and Google Sign-In.

## Privacy & Data

**Q: Does Twyn sell my data?**
A: No. Twyn does not sell, share, or monetise user data in any way.

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes. You can delete your account and all data from within the app or by submitting a request at twynapp.co.uk/delete-account.

**Q: How is my data protected?**
A: Sensitive data is encrypted and stored in a personal vault. Twyn uses consent-based data collection and minimal retention policies.

**Q: Does Twyn use my data to train AI models?**
A: No. Twyn does not use your data to train external AI models.

## Safety

**Q: How does Twyn handle harassment?**
A: Automatic content moderation in 80+ languages, user-accessible block and report tools, and progressive accountability for violations.

**Q: Can I block someone?**
A: Yes. You can block or report any profile at any time.

**Q: What is emergency data deletion?**
A: If you feel unsafe, you can trigger immediate deletion of your data from the app settings.

## Inclusivity

**Q: Is Twyn LGBTQ+ friendly?**
A: Yes. Twyn is inclusive of the full 2SLGBTQIA+ spectrum. Gender identity, sexuality, and romantic orientation are treated as separate, personal dimensions with full privacy controls.

**Q: Is Twyn accessible?**
A: Twyn is designed to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, with high contrast mode, reduced motion support, and screen reader compatibility.

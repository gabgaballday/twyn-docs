## About Twyn

Twyn is an AI dating app that creates a digital version of you — your AI Twin (or "Twyn") — through natural conversation. Your Twyn learns how you communicate, what you value, and what matters most to you in a partner, then works on your behalf to find genuinely compatible people.

Twyn is built on the belief that algorithms should strengthen connection rather than manipulate it. The app supports **81 languages**, is inclusive of the full 2SLGBTQIA+ spectrum, and is designed with accessibility at its core.
